About us

We are in an era of growing data, wherever you see there is data, from medicines to buying movie tickets to big tech companies like Microsoft, Google and Facebook. Understanding data and being able to use it in the right way is a difficult task!In response to the ever-increasing importance of data , Datazen is here to enhance the understanding, knowledge and experience of analyzing data, taking it step by step, talking to people from around the world and understanding from every perspective and every field. It is a place that gives students the platform to think out of the box and become as creative as they can get with data and create something from the very scratch. We organize activities that help the youth grow, have speaker sessions to gain current world knowledge and finally competitions to check one's progress and lastly a discord community that's available for everyone at all times to post their queries or achievements or just to interact with people who have the same interest as yours.
